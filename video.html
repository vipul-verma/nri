Bhoomi Mangalam

29th May, Nrityamanjari Festival 2016 Choreography by Sushant Maharana It is an ancient Vedic chant, a mantra of universal goodwill, recited to bring happiness, tranquility and peace to all. It signifies that may all elements in the Universe be in harmony, goodness, peace, love.
