About Us NRITYAMANJARI FESTIVAL

Nrityamanjari Festival

Nrityamanjari organizes Nrityamanjari Festival, an annual platform given to the students for them to understand their own strength and weakness. Stage has always been a perfect institution in itself that teaches a lot to these budding talents.

Nrityamanjari also gives platform to upcoming artists. Through Nrityamanjari festival, Manjari Odissi Nrityalaya ensures constant flow of new imaginative ideas, creativity along with traditional items. Preparing through the year, the program created by the dancers, musicians, choreographers,students and teachers. The teams along with the teachers collaborate with the stage designers,musicians, and light designers to create a visual spectacle.
