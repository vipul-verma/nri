Examination

Manjari Odissi Nrityalaya is affiliated to Pracheen Kala Kendra and is a registered examination Centre. It follows the course syllabus prescribed by the Kendra. The examinations are held twice in a year i.e. Annual and Bi-Annual (from Prarambhik Part-1 to Visharad final).

  • The Annual examinations are held during the month of December/January.
  • Bi-Annual examinations during the month of August/September every year.
  • Practical examinations are held in the first week of December.
S.No. COURSE DURATION
1 Prarambhik 2-Year preliminary course
2 Bhushan 3-Year course after Prarambik
3 Visharad 2-Year course after Bhushan

The courses for the examinations have been designed and graded after careful consideration and planning in collaboration with the recognized experts so that they meet both academic and professional requirements. Weightage is also given to methodical learning upholding the highest norms and standards on the lines of universities and examination boards. All the examinations of the Kendra are conducted on a date and time notified by the Kendra as per date-sheet of examinations through affiliated centers by appointing examiners (both theory and practical) under a well-guarded arrangement employing all the safeguards necessary for conducting such professional examinations.
