About Sushant

Sushant Maharana is a budding talent, a well-trained Odissi dancer. Dance has been an integral part of his life for the last 25 years. Sushant is the inheritor and proponent of his illustrious father Odissi maestro Guru Shri Trinath Maharana. He has been brought up in an atmosphere of dance and music. He has been trained under his father with deep dedication and discipline.

A dancer of vision and versatilit, Sushant is known for his intense, dynamic and energetic performance. His "Nritya" and "Abhinaya" have a proportionate blend of skill and commitment which reflects in his presentations. He is capable of taking dance to new heights. Sushant is committed towards undiluted and authentic approach to Odissi.

Sushant is a dedicated teacher of Manjari Odissi Nrityalaya; a school set up by Guru Shri Trinath Maharana that teaches Indian classical dance forms and music. He is founder and choreographer of a troupe - Nrityamanjari; a group of performing artists.

Highlights

  • Doordarshan Artist (B Grade).
  • SANGEET NATAK ACADEMI scholarship holder for Chhau Dance.
  • Performed in SANGEET NATAK AKADEMI & ICCR shows.
  • Founder of NRITYAMANRAJI Dance Group.
  • Dance Director of MANJARI ODISSI NRITYALAYA.

Training

  • Sushant got his training in Odissi dance from his father Odissi maestro Guru Trinath Maharana.
  • The credit of Sushant's vigorous acrobatic movements in his dance performance goes to his expertise in Chhau dance under the tutelage of Sangeet Natak Akademi awardee - Guru Shri Shashadhar Acharya.
  • In addition to Odissi and Chhau, he has also been trained in Kathak by Late Guru Shri R.S. Verma.

Achievements

Sushant Maharana has been a scholarship holder in Chhau dance from Sangeet Natak Akademi. He is also a Doordarshan artist. He has performed extensively in various programmes in India and abroad - Switzerland, Canada, London, France, Myanmar and South Korea.
